Fund Profile

Aim

Income and capital growth from a globally diversified portfolio of hard-currency sovereign and quasi-sovereign bonds.

Investment Case

We buy the bonds of wealthy creditor nations that the market still misprices because of a decades-old "emerging market" label. Strong sovereigns — with taxing power, infinite life and real national wealth — should trade tighter than corporates, not wider. That mispricing is the opportunity.

Process

The Fund invests only in countries with government debt below 100% of GDP and net foreign assets better than −50% of GDP. All holdings are hard-currency (USD/EUR); any euro exposure is hedged back to US dollars, leaving effectively zero FX risk at the fund level.

Portfolio

A high-conviction, investment-grade sovereign and quasi-sovereign strategy, diversified across creditor nations in Latin America, the Gulf, Asia and beyond.

Investment Objective

The fund is actively managed without reference to a benchmark or comparative index for performance comparison purposes.

For the avoidance of doubt, if you decide to invest, you will be buying units/shares in the Fund and will not be investing directly in the underlying assets of the Fund.
